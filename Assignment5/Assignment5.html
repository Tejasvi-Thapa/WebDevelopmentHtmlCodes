Captain America

Captain America is a fictional superhero from the Marvel Comics and the deuteragonist in the Marvel Universe. He made his first appearance in the comic book Captain America Comics #1 in 1941. He wears a suit that has a white star on his chest with 2 stripes (still white) on each side next to the star. His suit is blue.
